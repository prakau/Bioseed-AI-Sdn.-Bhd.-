FAQ

Answering key questions about our technology and mission.

What is agri-biotech?

Agri-biotech (Agricultural Biotechnology) is the use of scientific tools and techniques, including genetic engineering, AI, and nano-innovations, to improve plants, animals, and microorganisms. At Bioseed AI, we use it to create climate-resilient crops, more effective biofertilizers, and intelligent farming systems.

How does nano-technology help farming?

Our nano-innovations, like BioSynth Prime™ and GrowNano Boost™, work at a microscopic level. They enable precision delivery of nutrients, massive improvements in water retention (50-80x), and even boost photosynthesis. This means farmers use less fertilizer and water, save money, and get higher, more nutritious yields.

Is FarmAssist™ difficult to use for rural farmers?

No. FarmAssist™ is designed with a simple, farmer-friendly interface. It's multilingual and, most importantly, **offline-first**. This means farmers can get critical data and AI-powered advice even in areas with poor or no internet connectivity. We also offer a Freemium model to ensure smallholders can access it.

Where are your products tested?

All our products undergo rigorous field validation. We have active pilot trials across Malaysia, India, and with global partner farms. This real-world testing ensures our technology delivers proven results in diverse climates and soil conditions for crops like maize, rice, wheat, and more.
